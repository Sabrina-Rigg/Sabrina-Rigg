# Hi, I’m Sabrina 👋

I’m a junior full-stack developer based in New Zealand, with a background in small business ownership, team leadership, and building practical solutions for real people.

Before moving into tech, I ran and managed a small café - hiring and leading staff, working hands-on in fast-paced environments, and growing a community-focused business through challenging times (including COVID). That experience shaped how I work today: calmly, collaboratively, and with a strong sense of ownership.

## Why tech?

I’m drawn to software development because it allows me to turn real-world problems into useful tools. I care about ethical tech, sustainability, and building things that genuinely help people.

During my time at Dev Academy, I developed full-stack skills and discovered how much I enjoy working in teams, learning continuously, and shaping ideas into user-focused applications. My final group project combined two of my biggest passions — technology and self-sufficiency, through an app that helps people grow food sustainably based on location, season, and family needs.
